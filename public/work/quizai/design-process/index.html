Ideation

During the ideation phase, I explored multiple quiz formats, learning styles, and user paths—but my goal remained clear: to create the most accessible and easy-to-use learning experience possible.

A key addition was the AI-powered summary generator, designed to simplify complex notes and readings into bite-sized, easy-to-understand formats.

This not only saved users valuable time but also helped them grasp core concepts faster and retain them longer, especially before quizzes or exams.

It was particularly useful for students who struggled with long texts—giving them the confidence to keep up without feeling overwhelmed.

Color Palette

This project adopted a minimalistic visual style, using ASU's official color palette to reflect the academic identity and maintain brand consistency. I incorporated maroon and gold as primary colors to foster energy, motivation, and trust among student users. I also followed the 60–30–10 rule to achieve better visual balance and clarity across the UI.
