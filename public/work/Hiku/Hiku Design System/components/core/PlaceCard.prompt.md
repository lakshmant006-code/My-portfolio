PlaceCard — imagery-forward listing card for the Explore / Shop feeds.

```jsx
<PlaceCard image={photo} title="Kachina Trailhead Camp"
  meta="Flagstaff, AZ · 3.2 mi" rating={4.92} price="$32" priceUnit="night" />
```

Rounded photo with a heart save toggle (top-right), then bold title, muted meta, star rating and price. Grid them 2-up on wider screens.
