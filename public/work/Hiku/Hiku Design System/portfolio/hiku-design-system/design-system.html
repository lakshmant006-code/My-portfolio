Hiku

Design system — trail tracking, camping & gear for hiking enthusiasts

Hiku's identity is earthy, vibrant and calm — deep forest greens on generous white space, the Satoshi typeface throughout, Lucide line icons, fully-rounded pills and 12 px cards, and flat monochrome-green nature illustration. This page documents the foundations and components that power the Hiku mobile app prototype.

Brand illustration

Flat, layered, vibrant

Illustrations are flat vector landscapes in the brand greens. The system also generates six monochrome hiking scenes (mountains, tent, forest, trail, campfire, lake) built only from the green palette — used for empty states, headers, and as the graceful fallback behind real photography.

Interactive prototype

The Hiku mobile app

A full click-through built on these foundations: splash → snow-reveal loading → Explore (filter popups), Trails (live hiking map with tap-to-route navigation), Community (expandable clubs), Register (confetti), Shop (browse → cart → checkout), and Account.
